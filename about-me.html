About Me

Struggling to keep up with your

I’m Reena—a social media manager, strategist, UGC creator, and content creator. Before I found my passion for social media, I spent years captivating audiences as a TV soap opera actor and anchoring events for Fortune 500 companies like Apple, Samsung, and Toyota. With a background in hotel management (specializing in marketing) and coming from a family of business owners, I deeply understand the value of every business and the effort it takes to make it thrive.

Our Story

At Socials by Reena, we’ve worked with over 200 businesses, ranging from global brands like HelloFresh and BlendJet to local treasures like Patel’s Kingston Lanes. For Patel’s Kingston Lanes, we took their social media from zero presence to becoming a recognized name in their local community.

Our content isn’t just pretty—it’s powerful. We’ve helped clients achieve over 3+ million views and significant revenue from single videos. Our content strategies deliver real results, like CTRs and clicks ranking in the top 31% of the industry average. And because I’m an all-niche creator, I don’t believe in narrowing down. Instead, I conduct in-depth research to understand your brand and become your niche.

We constantly stay ahead of the curve, keeping ourselves updated with the latest social media trends and strategies. From algorithm changes to content best practices, we make sure your brand is always a step ahead in the digital space.
